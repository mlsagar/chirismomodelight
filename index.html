Bringing Himalaya's soul food to Boulder

For delicious, handmade steamed momos (Himalayan dumplings), there's no better spot in Boulder, CO than Chiri's Momo Delight. Get a plate of momos or a bowl of noodles for cravable comfort you'll want to try again and again.

We’re proud to be one of Boulder’s original food trucks!
